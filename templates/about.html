About Us

Your go-to platform for real-time sports updates, insights, and fan engagement.

Our Mission

At SportsTracker, our mission is to bring fans closer to the action. We aim to provide live scores, detailed stats, and timely updates in a clean, intuitive interface — so you never miss a moment.

Our Team

We are a small but passionate team of sports lovers, developers, and designers. We work together to build tools that turn raw data into meaningful insights. Our backgrounds span software development, data analytics, sports journalism, and UX design.

What We Value

  • Accuracy: Timely and correct information is our top priority.
  • Transparency: We believe in open data sources and clear methodology.
  • Community: Fans, players, coaches — we’re all part of this ecosystem.
  • Growth: We continuously improve and expand to new sports, leagues, and features.
